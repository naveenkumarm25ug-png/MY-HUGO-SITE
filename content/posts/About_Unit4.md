**In this unit, we were working on individual projects which helped us to understand the true importance of communication in learning and development. This project was not simply based on rubrics; Instead it was based on how we can communicate information, efforts that we are putting on it and self-learning. We chose to work on a chatbot and a dashboard project, which required us to explore, research and solve challenges that we had faced in those projects.**
**During the chatbot project, communication played an important role because it helped me to organize ideas and  present information clearly. A chatbot mainly designed to communicate with users effectively, so that I understood how users can expect answers to those questions they ask. and also I made sure the responses were understandable and meaningful and  useful. This helped me think from the user’s perspective and I understood  the way information is shared clearly and effectively.**
**Similarly, while working on the dashboard project, I’ve learnt how we can communicate information through different types of charts and graphs. This project insists us to make reliable and communicable graphs and charts which are taken from the complex data. So we can learn how to communicate complex information in an easy way.**
**Through these projects, I realized that communication is not only about speaking but also presenting ideas, sharing information effectively to the users. This journey helped me improve both my technical knowledge and my ability to communicate information clearly to the users. Hope you enjoyed my blog :)**
