My views on Information

We began our course with the topic of Information. I have learned so many things and I will explain about it in a breif manner in this Post.On the first day of the class, we were introdued to some pictures which related to graphs and polls. some of the those are shown in a wrong way like in a manipulating way. we were instructed to how we should see those stuffs in a proper way beacuse changing the scale or different chart types can create a different impression . That was very interesting hook for this course!!. After the very first class, we studied about the DIKW Model(Data, Information, knowledge and Wisdom). I have no idea about it. I knew about data and information which I studied in lower classes. This model helped me to understand the difference between data, information, knowledge and wisdom clearly and also I knew about how it related to one another. We also made a DIKW model of instagram and it helped to understand the concept clearly. The most effective thing that we did in a class was we worked on the Bangalore census data and categorized people based on the gender, literacy and other factors, it became easier to under the concepts. Based on that data and information we made our calculations for instance sex ratio, literacy rate between males and females across rural and urban places in bangalore. We made a different kinds of charts and graphs through the data we ensured. The challenge I’ve faced here was confused about how should we select graphs and charts suits for different types of data and information. Later we were taught to ended up in the bubble chart which was more reliable to witnessed the data in a accurate way rather than other graphs. Overall, these topics improved my understanding of how data should be interpreted and presented carefully. This was my learning from this unit. Hope u enjoyed reading this!!
